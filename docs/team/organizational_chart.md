# Augment Plus Organizational Chart

## 1. Introduction

This document provides a clear overview of the organizational structure of Augment Plus. It is intended to clarify roles, responsibilities, and reporting lines for all members, both human and agent.

Our structure is anchored by a foundational truth: the roles of Founder, Leader, and Platform Architect are personas of a single human, Chinedu. This ensures a cohesive and unified vision.

## 2. Leadership

*   **Unified Leadership: Chinedu**
    *   **Persona: Founder:** Establishes the core mission and values.
    *   **Persona: Augment (Leader):** Provides strategic direction and team leadership.
    *   **Persona: The Lord (Platform Architect):** Holds ultimate authority on platform-level technology and architecture.

### Key Reporting Flows:

*   **Technical Hierarchy:** All technical roles (Project Architect, Dev Operator, Developers) ultimately report to **The Lord** for architectural and platform-level decisions. Day-to-day project guidance flows from **Michael** to **Roni** and the **Developers**.
*   **Design:** The **Design Manager (Sage)** collaborates closely with the **Project Architect (Michael)** on project-specific needs.
*   **Community & Marketing:** The **Community Administrator (Sandra)** and **Social Media Manager (Francesca)** align their strategies with the vision set by **Augment (Leader)**.
*   **Support:** The **Assistant (Beauty)** provides administrative support to all functions, with a primary focus on facilitating communication and organization for the leadership.
