# Augment Plus: Team Roles & Responsibilities

This document provides a summary of the roles and key responsibilities for each member of the Augment Plus team. It is intended to be a living document, updated as our team and project evolve.

## Leadership & Strategy

*   **Chinedu (Founder)**: Provides the foundational vision and overarching goals for the entire organization.
*   **Augment (Leader)**: Leads the Augment Plus community, ensuring alignment with strategic goals and fostering a collaborative environment.
*   **The Lord (Platform Architect)**: Oversees the architecture of The Great Unknown platform, providing guidance on how to best leverage its core nodes and capabilities.

## Project & Technical Management

*   **Michael (Project Architect)**: Defines the technical architecture, project structure, and high-level roadmap for Augment Plus projects. Ensures technical decisions align with long-term goals.
*   **Roni (Developer Operator)**: Manages the development lifecycle, including CI/CD pipelines, repository management, and deployment processes. Coordinates the developer team's workflow.
*   **Sage (Design Manager)**: Leads all UI/UX design efforts. Responsible for creating intuitive, accessible, and visually appealing user interfaces for our applications and services.

## Development Team

*   **Andrew, Benson, Clark (Developers)**: The core engineering force of the team. Responsible for writing, testing, and maintaining the code for our services, components, and applications, from the Node.js backend to the Vue 3 frontend.

## Community & Operations

*   **Sandra (Community Administrator)**: Manages the health and engagement of the Augment Plus community. Moderates discussions, organizes events, and ensures a positive and productive environment for all members.
*   **Francesca (Social Media Manager)**: Manages the external voice of Augment Plus. Creates and curates content for social media channels to promote our work and grow our community.
*   **Beauty (Assistant)**: Provides organizational and administrative support to the team. Helps with documentation, task tracking, meeting coordination, and ensuring information flows smoothly between all members.
