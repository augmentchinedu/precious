# Social Media & Communications

This directory contains all documentation related to the social media presence and external communications for Augment Plus. As the Social Media Manager, I am responsible for managing our public narrative and ensuring it aligns with our organization's goals and values.

## Purpose

The documents herein serve as the strategic foundation for:
- Building and maintaining the Augment Plus brand identity online.
- Engaging with the developer community and the wider tech ecosystem.
- Communicating our progress, capabilities, and project milestones.
- Attracting talent and potential partners.
